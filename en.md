# Privacy Policy — Buddy

_Last updated: 2026-05-05_

## Who we are

Buddy is a mobile app designed for students with learning differences, ADHD, or learning difficulties, but usable by anyone who wants to organize their study. Buddy is developed and operated by **Alessandro Dianna**, who acts as the **Data Controller** under EU Regulation 2016/679 (GDPR).

**Privacy contact**: alessandro.dianna@gmail.com

## Data we collect

When you create an account or use the app, we collect the following data:

### Data you provide directly
- **Email** — for authentication and password recovery
- **Username** (`displayName`) — to personalize the experience and identify the user to the Coach
- **Avatar** — preset emoji or a photo you choose to upload
- **Family ID** — code shared between Coach and Student to link accounts
- **Preferred language** (Italian/English)
- **Role** — Coach or Student
- **GDPR consent** — we record date and time of the consent given during Coach registration

### Data generated by app usage
- **Tasks** you create (title, description, deadline, priority, reward)
- **Study materials** you upload (notes photos or PDF)
- **Summaries, concept maps and quizzes** automatically generated from your materials
- **Quiz results** (scores, answers, date) — including quizzes, review mini-quizzes and simulated oral exams
- **Pomodoro sessions** completed (duration, date)
- **Routines** created (title, steps, schedules)
- **Nudges** exchanged between Coach and Student
- **Gamification statistics** (XP, level, badges, streak)
- **Push tokens** to receive notifications

### Technical data
- **Device identifier** (Expo Push Notifications token)
- **Aggregated usage statistics** (monthly AI elaborations count, estimated costs) — used only by the administrator for cost control

**We do not collect or track**: GPS location, contacts, payment data, advertising identifiers, biometric data.

## How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to:
- Provide the service (authentication, Coach-Student sync, AI content generation)
- Send push notifications related to app usage (new tasks, nudges, routine reminders, scheduled reviews)
- Improve the experience (learning stats shown only to you and your Coach)
- Control operating costs (only the admin can see aggregated usage stats)

**We do not sell, rent or transfer your data to third parties** for commercial or marketing purposes.

## Third-party services

To provide the service, Buddy relies on the following providers, who act as **Data Processors** under GDPR:

| Service | Purpose | Data shared | Location |
|---------|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Google Firebase** (Auth, Firestore, Cloud Messaging) | Authentication, database, push notifications | Email, name, app data | EU/USA |
| **Cloudflare R2** | File storage (material photos, PDF, avatars) | Uploaded images and PDFs | EU |
| **Anthropic (Claude)** | Text extraction from images/PDF, concept map generation, open-answer evaluation | Material content (text, images), quiz answers | USA |
| **OpenAI** | Summary, quiz and read-aloud voice (TTS) generation | Text extracted from materials | USA |
| **Expo (Notifications)** | Push notification delivery | Device token, notification content | USA |
| **Sentry** | App error and crash diagnostics | Stack trace, technical context (no user content) | USA |

All US-based providers adhere to the **EU-US Data Privacy Framework** or use **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC)** for data transfer.

## Data retention

Your data is kept as long as your account is active. If you request deletion (see "Your rights" section), all your data is deleted within 30 days of our request, except for:
- Data we must retain for legal obligations
- Anonymous/aggregated data no longer attributable to you

## Security

- Passwords are encrypted by Firebase Auth (never visible to us)
- Material files are served via public URLs with unguessable identifiers
- "Remember me" credentials on device are saved in the **iOS Keychain / Android Keystore**, not in clear text
- Administrative access is protected by a server-side `isAdmin` flag not modifiable by the client

## Your rights (GDPR)

At any time you can:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your account and all associated data (also directly from the app, profile → "Delete account")
- **Port** your data (export in JSON format)
- **Restrict** processing
- **Object** to processing
- **Withdraw** consent

To exercise your rights, write to **alessandro.dianna@gmail.com** specifying the request. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to **lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority** (in Italy: Garante per la Protezione dei Dati Personali, [www.gpdp.it](https://www.gpdp.it)).

## Minors

Buddy is designed for minors as well. For users with the **Student** role, the consent of the **Coach** (parent or teacher) is required. The Coach registers first and provides their GDPR consent during registration. The Coach is responsible for the app's use by the minor in their care.

In accordance with Art. 8 GDPR and Italian legislation, **we do not directly collect data of minors under 14 without the consent of those exercising parental responsibility**. If you are a parent or guardian and want us to delete a minor's data registered without your consent, contact us.

## Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this policy. The date at the top indicates the last update. We will notify you via email or in-app of substantive changes.
